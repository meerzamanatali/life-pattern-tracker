## 📖 What is Life Pattern Tracker?

Life Pattern Tracker is a personal web app that helps you understand how you actually spend your time every day. You log your activities throughout the day — from studying to scrolling Instagram — and the app turns that data into honest insights about your habits, energy, and behaviour patterns.

It is not a to-do list. It is not a calendar. It is a mirror — one that shows you the truth about your day that your memory never could.

---

## 🧠 The Problem It Solves

Most people want to improve themselves but have no idea where to actually start. Why? Because they rely on memory — and memory is unreliable, biased, and forgets the patterns that matter most.

**Here is what you cannot know without tracking:**

- You think you are productive in the morning — but your data shows your real focus peaks at 2pm
- You think you only spend 20 minutes on Instagram — but the truth is closer to 90 minutes
- You feel tired at the end of the day but cannot explain why — until you see that every social media session leaves your energy lower than before
- You know your good days feel different from bad days — but you cannot explain what actually makes them good

Life Pattern Tracker solves this by giving you a structured, honest, data-driven picture of your day — every single day.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

It is as simple as three steps:

### Step 1 — Log
Open the app whenever you start or finish an activity. Tap the activity from the quick-tap grid, set the start and end time, and optionally add context like your energy level, mood, or what triggered the activity. Done in under 15 seconds.

### Step 2 — Track
Every entry you log builds your personal dataset. The Today screen shows you a live view of your day with stats and a colour-coded timeline. After a few weeks the Insights screen starts showing you patterns you never knew existed.

### Step 3 — Improve
With real data in hand you can make real decisions. Move your study sessions to your peak focus hours. Identify and eliminate the triggers that pull you to social media. Replicate the conditions of your best days. Improve deliberately — not randomly.

## 🎯 Use Cases

**For students:**
Track study sessions, university classes, and social media use side by side. See how much time actually goes into learning vs distractions. Discover which subjects get your best focus and at what time of day.

**For self-learners:**
Log every ML study session, coding project, or book reading with the topic and how well you understood it. Build a clear picture of your learning progress over weeks and months.

**For anyone building better habits:**
Track what triggers your unproductive behaviour. See whether your daily goal is being met. Compare this week to last week and see if you are actually improving or just feeling like you are.

**For people who feel busy but unproductive:**
Find out exactly where your hours disappear. Most people are shocked by the gap between how they think they spend their time and how they actually do.

## ✨ Features

### Quick Logging
- Large tap-friendly preset grid for the most common daily activities
- Activities include Instagram, YouTube, ML Study, Coding, University, Part-time Job, Reading, Exercise, Eating and Cooking, Cleaning, Sleep and Rest
- Times auto-chain so each new entry starts where the last one ended
- Optional context fields — energy level before and after, mood, what triggered the activity, where you were, which device you used, whether it was planned or impulsive

### Today Screen
- See everything you have logged today in one clean view
- Summary cards showing total logged time, productive time, social media time and number of entries
- Colour-coded timeline so you can see your day at a glance
- Highlights gaps in your day where time is unaccounted for
- End of day summary — log your wake time, sleep quality, overall day rating and whether you hit your daily goal

### Insights Screen
Four tabs of data that get more powerful the longer you use the app:

**This Week** — A chart showing how your time was split across categories every day this week. See your productive hours, social media hours and regret rate at a glance. Your top 3 activities by time are shown below.

**Heatmap** — A grid showing your most active hours across every day of the week across all your history. Instantly see when you are most productive and when social media tends to creep in.

**Week vs Week** — Compare this week to last week across learning hours, work hours, social media hours, average day rating and goals met. Trend arrows show whether you are improving or slipping.

**Patterns** — Six deep insights that expose the truth about your behaviour:
- What triggers your social media use most
- Which activities give you energy and which drain you
- How different activities affect your mood
- Your best day template — what your highest rated days have in common
- Which location gives you the best focus
- How much of your time is planned vs impulsive

### Settings
- Customise your quick-tap preset grid
- Set a daily goal and track progress toward it on the Today screen
- Switch between Light, Dark and System theme
- Export all your data as a CSV file anytime
- View your account details and usage stats

## 📱 Device Support

| Device | Experience |
|--------|-----------|
| 📱 Mobile | Primary logging experience — large tap targets, bottom navigation, installable as a home screen app |
| 🖥️ Desktop | Full analytics experience — wider layout, top navigation, side by side charts and insights |

The app is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** — meaning you can install it directly to your phone home screen without going through any app store. It works and feels like a native app.

**To install on iPhone:** Open in Safari → tap Share → Add to Home Screen

**To install on Android:** Open in Chrome → tap the browser menu → Add to Home Screen

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

Your data is completely private. This is not just a promise — it is enforced at the database level.

- Every user has their own completely separate private space
- It is technically impossible for one user to see another user's data
- No third-party analytics or tracking scripts are used anywhere in the app
- No ads, ever
- You can export and download all your personal data as a CSV file at any time from the Settings screen
- You can permanently delete all your data at any time from the Settings screen
